What is Light?

Have you ever wondered where light comes from or what it actually is? Light surrounds us every day, helping us see the world, but its true nature is even more fascinating. It's not just something we see -- it's a form of energy that travels incredibly fast, allowing us to experience everything from sunlight to the glow of a lightbulb.
